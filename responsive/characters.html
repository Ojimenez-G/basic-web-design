carnage: cletus kasady

portrayed by: Woody Harrelson

A deranged serial killer, Cletus Kasady bonds with a red symbiote spawn of Venom, transforming into the violent and unpredictable Carnage. Unlike Venom, Carnage thrives on chaos and destruction, making him one of the deadliest enemies Eddie Brock has ever faced. With no moral code and enhanced abilities, Carnage is a force of pure madness.
