venom the anti hero

Venom is a popular character in the Marvel Universe, first appearing in The Amazing Spider-Man comic in 1988. He is an alien symbiote, a type of creature that bonds with a host, and when it merges with Eddie Brock, a reporter with a grudge against Spider-Man, they form a powerful being known as Venom. This new creature has super strength, agility, and can heal quickly. Venom also has the ability to shape-shift and create weapons from the symbiote. Though he started as a villain, Venom often acts as an anti-hero, sometimes protecting people and fighting other dangerous enemies. His creepy appearance, with a large mouth full of sharp teeth and a white spider symbol on his chest, has made him one of Spider-Mans most famous and interesting foes.
